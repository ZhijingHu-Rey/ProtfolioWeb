3D Grabbing Platform and Grabbing Method
based on Deep Learning

Advisor

Group Memebers

About the Invention

To complete the designed robot in short time with low cost and high precision, the 3D printing method would be our best manufacturing method in the laboratory. The printing material is PLA (polylactic acid), with 17.5mm in diameter.

The grabbing hand is designed in three pieces of grabbing claws. Instead of two, it has much more better grabbing effect facing different shapes of the object. The design of limiting position self-locking allows the gripper to better grip objects without relying on the steering gear. The simple structure gives it high reliability and low cost, and the use of connecting rod makes it easy to scale, providing strong modification potential and wide space for improvement and optimization.

The entire system consists of 5 sub-systems: one control system, one driving belt system(grabbing platform), one robot system(grabbing system), one acquisition system and one object type and pose identification system. The simple version of the grabbing performance is using 8cm x 8cm cube with three different color in red, green, and blue as the target objects, and the different poses and velocities of them will be generated through the platform.

The control system is used for controlling the driving belt system to move along a track, and the acquisition system will acquire and preprocess the original three-dimensional point cloud data in a visual field, then later outputing them to the next sub-system, the object type and pose identification system

The object type and pose identification system will identify three-dimensional point cloud data of the target object by using identification model based on a deep learning network, determining the type of object and its corresponding pose information. The outputs about the specific information will be sent to the control system. Once the data is received, the control system will plan a moving path according to the recognition result, completing the moving and grabbing actions.

The project is later worked on and improved by Zhejiang University, and the invention now has successfully acknowledged and became a granted patent.
